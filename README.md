# CONTENT
For this project I want to train multiple models on a dataset by Economist Reinhart. The Dataset specifically focuses on Banking,Debt,Financial,inflation and systematic crises that occurred from 1860 to 2014 in 13 African countries. The 13 African countries include: Algeria, Angola,Central African Republic, Ivory Coast, Egypt, Kenya, Mauritius, Morocco,Nigeria, South Africa, Tunisia, Zambia and Zimbabwe.
# INTRO ON THE OWNER OF DATASET
Reinhart is known for her research on historical financial crises,prominently featured in her book This Time is Different;Eight Centuries Of Financial Folly, which details the historical patterns and consequences of such events.  Her data and research are often cited in the IMF Global financial stability report.
